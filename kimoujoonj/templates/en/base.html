Kim Ou-joon

Kim Ou-joon (1968. 12. 4 ~ ) is a journalist of Korea. In 1998 he founded "Ddanzi Ilbo," the very first internet based newspaper in the world. In 2011 he launched the podcast talkshow "Naneun Ggomsuda," or “I’m a Petty-Minded Creep,” which is regarded to have opened the podcast talkshow industry. He is now hosting a daily radio show, "Kim Ou-joon's News Factory," which is picked as Korean people's favorite and regarded to have a huge impact on Korean politics.
